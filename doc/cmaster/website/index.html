CMaster Channel Services

Welcome to the Homepages of the "CMaster" - The Undernet Channel Services server!

Development on the service began in October 2000 and was a joint venture between the Coder Comittee and Channel Service Commitee. It has almost been running a year now, and has developed considerably since the first version.

The current version is 1.1pl2, available from CVS.
